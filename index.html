02 — The Protocol

Making Reality Programmable

BCCS replaces the PDF Oracle with a continuous, cryptographic control plane for biological capital. Three primitives power the system:

BAIN ID

The ISIN for Nature

A 21-character immutable identifier for every biological asset. Encodes asset type, jurisdiction, biome, and registration. Anchored on Base. Non-transferable. Designed to evolve from proprietary stewardship into a consortium-governed reference standard.

8-STATE ENGINE

Thermodynamic Lifecycle

Every BAIN ID moves through 8 states: Registered, Active, Suspended, Migrating, Dormant, Degraded, Depleted, Archived. Real-time physical state tracking from verification to terminal entropy.

PoPS

Proof-of-Physical-State

A novel DePIN consensus mechanism. Validator Nodes cross-reference Oracle claims against satellite imagery, thermal data, and independent research streams. Three-tier verification — remote sensing, ground sensor networks, and accredited physical inspection — combined through a confidence scoring model and dispute-resolution framework. Fraudulent claims are algorithmically slashed.

BIOCLEARING

Biological Settlement

Protocol-native clearing mechanism. When a BAIN ID transitions state, the engine calculates net change in biological capital value and settles on-chain. Real-time, transparent, composable.
