The Problem
The agentic economy has no bridge to biological reality

Over 50 million machine-to-machine transactions have been processed. Agent wallets, payment rails, and policy engines are being built at extraordinary speed. But this entire infrastructure assumes digital assets — binary, deterministic, instantly verifiable. The majority of Earth's real economic value is biological, analog, and thermodynamically unstable.

Architecture
Five components. One control plane.

BCCS sits between biological reality and digital infrastructure. Every component is designed for machine consumption.

01
BAIN ID
Immutable Bio-Asset Identification Number. Like ISIN for securities — but for forests, permafrost, soil, and ecosystems. 21-character canonical format.
02
8-State Engine
Formal state machine: Observed → Registered → Verified → Standardized → Insured → Final. Plus Reversed (degradation) and Depleted (terminal).
03
Oracle Network
Accredited physical verification fabric. Credentialed scientists and sensor networks providing ground-truth attestation under protocol rules.
04
Policy Engine
Machine-readable authorization layer. Tells autonomous agents what they may do with a bio-asset, in its current state, under what constraints.
05
API Surface
Standard endpoints for agent wallets, payment rails, treasury systems, insurance platforms, and sovereign registries. Infrastructure-agnostic.
